About the artist:

Yuriy Khymych (1928–2003), a classic of 20th century Ukrainian fine arts, was a master of architectural landscape and graphic arts.
He was recognized as an Honored Artist and Architect of Ukraine, as well as an Honored Artist of the USSR.
He was a member of the National Union of Architects (from 1955) and of the National Union of Artists (from 1962).
In the late 1950s he gained renown among art historians as one of the USSR’s premier watercolorists.
He spent decades teaching and was also a Reserve Captain of Military Engineering.
